.. title:: Download CINEMAS 

========================
How to download CINEMAS
========================



Thanks for showing your interest in downloading CINEMAS software.

`Click here <https://ikst.res.in/cinemas/download>`_ to download the latest version of CINEMAS, for your choice of OS: Windows, Linux or MAC-OS.

You can also post your questions on CINEMAS `discussion-forum <https://ikst.res.in/cinemas/discussion-forum>`_.
